Photography: My Fav Hobby


Photography

I picked up photography when I was 15, and it quickly became my biggest hobby and creative outlet. I was able to capture funny moments with my friends and create art with it. I've been able to turn it into a side hustle as well, with keeping it a hobby, too.

I shoot with a Nikon D5200, and a Nikon EM film camera. I mostly take portrait photos.

Portrait of a friend at the Sand Dunes at night

My Camera Gear

  • Nikon D5200 DSLR camera body
  • Nikon EM film camera
  • 24-70mm zoom lens
  • Tripod
  • Adobe Lightroom

Types of Photography I Love

  1. Landscape Photography
    • Mountains, hiking trails, lakes
    • Sunsets (can't beat them!)
  2. Street Photography
    • Candids of streetgoers
    • Night photography in old towns/centers
  3. Portrait Photography
    • Candids of friends
    • Flash photography in lower light settings
